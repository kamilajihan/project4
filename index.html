Phineas and Ferb

Written by Jihan

Phineas and Ferb is an American animated television comedy and Disney Channel Original Series produced by Disney Television Animation for Disney Channel. The first episode premiered on August 17, 2007. The show is an Emmy Award-winning American animated television series about two young stepbrothers who turn their dreams into reality every day. Their teenage sister is jealous and tries to get them into trouble, but the evidence always disappears before their mom sees it. Meanwhile, an evil yet oddly eccentric scientist, Dr. Doofenshmirtz, is trying to wreak havoc in the Tri-State Area, and only the small pet of the two kids named Perry, also known as Agent P, can stop him.
